Eat Around, Not On Top

Save image ↓
The advice isn't wrong — it's the execution that trips people up. “Eat more protein” quietly becomes “eat more food” when we add shakes, bars, and snacks on top of what we're already eating. The better move? Build your plate around protein instead of stacking it on top. Make it the foundation, not the extra. The total intake might not change much. The composition changes significantly. Try it this week: before adding anything to a meal, check whether the protein is already there as the anchor. If it isn't, rearrange rather than add. #nutrition #protein #healthyhabits #coaching https://crawford-coaching.ca/writing/15-becoming-a-snacker
